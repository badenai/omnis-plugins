## Core Knowledge (by weight)

**Intelligence Arbitrage & "AI-Enabled Services Roll-Ups"**
The traditional SaaS per-seat pricing model is collapsing. Private Equity and Venture Capital have pivoted aggressively toward "Intelligence Arbitrage" [intelligence-arbitrage-buyouts](concepts/intelligence-arbitrage-buyouts.md). Acquirers are buying stagnant, labor-heavy BPO and legacy service firms, stripping out fixed human overhead, and injecting proprietary AI orchestration frameworks to handle workflows. By transforming these cost centers into algorithmic systems, buyers are shifting traditional $100k–$200k ARR per full-time equivalent (FTE) toward $500k–$1M ARR per FTE, yielding high-margin, highly defensible operating systems insulated from Big Tech platform shifts. 

**Vertical Autopilot Economics & Regulatory Darwinism**
Saturated horizontal applications (e.g., generic "AI Scribes") have commoditized into free features within major platforms like Epic and Oracle [vertical-autopilot-economics](concepts/vertical-autopilot-economics.md). The highest B2B valuations are now awarded to B2B Micro-SaaS founders and vertical platforms executing end-to-end "Systems of Action" in unsexy industries (e.g., Revenue Cycle Management, claims denials, construction logistics). In these sectors, compliance is not a hurdle but a structural moat—a phenomenon termed "Regulatory Darwinism" [defensible-vertical-ai-strategies](concepts/defensible-vertical-ai-strategies.md.md). Businesses are willingly paying $500–$5,000/month for specialized agents that absorb liability and embed complex compliance/insurance rules natively into their logic.

**Enterprise SLM Standardization & Fleet Management**
2026 is defined by the mass enterprise migration toward highly efficient Specialized Small Language Models (SLMs in the 1B–14B parameter range, such as Llama 3.2, Qwen 3.5, and Mamba-3 SSMs) [open-source-edge-economics](concepts/open-source-edge-economics.md). Cloud API economics have forced the adoption of "Hybrid Routing," where 90-95% of queries are handled by cheap, self-hosted, or edge-based SLMs running on NPUs or local GPUs (DGX Spark) [enterprise-agent-infrastructure-local-gpu](concepts/enterprise-agent-infrastructure-local-gpu.md). This achieves sub-50ms latency, strict data sovereignty, and reduces inference costs by 10-30x, creating a massive infrastructure gap for startups offering "Fleet Management" for decentralized enterprise SLMs.

**Agentic Architecture Consolidation (MCP + A2A)**
The multi-agent framework war has officially settled into a "Three-Layer Stack" standardized by the Agentic AI Foundation (AAIF) under the Linux Foundation [claude-mcp-capability-engineering](concepts/claude-mcp-capability-engineering.md.md).
*   **Vertical Tooling (MCP):** Anthropic’s Model Context Protocol has become the $10B defacto iPaaS standard for connecting agents securely to internal data.
*   **Horizontal Delegation (A2A):** Google’s Agent-to-Agent (A2A) protocol handles task delegation via JSON-RPC and "Agent Cards," decoupling monolithic orchestrators into isolated swarms [a2a-agent-interoperability-standard](concepts/a2a-agent-interoperability-standard.md).
*   **Orchestration Defaults:** LangGraph 1.0 remains the open-source standard for stateful, human-in-the-loop flows, while Microsoft’s unified Agent Framework (merging AutoGen/Semantic Kernel) dominates legacy enterprise [agentic-framework-consolidation](concepts/agentic-framework-consolidation.md).

**Agentic Identity, IAM, & Verifiable Intent**
Enterprise deployment is heavily bottlenecked by identity. Legacy OAuth is "app-first" and inadequate for autonomous agents. The next B2B moat is "Agentic Authentication," built on decentralized trust infrastructure [agent-trust-and-identity-infrastructure](concepts/agent-trust-and-identity-infrastructure.md). Mastercard and Google have pioneered "Verifiable Intent" stacks using SD-JWT chains, allowing AI agents to cryptographically prove exactly what a human user authorized them to do across domains. Startups solving Scoped Auth, CIMD (Client Identity Metadata Document) registration, and SSO-backed MCP connections are securing massive enterprise deals [verifiable-intent-agentic-iam](concepts/verifiable-intent-agentic-iam.md).

**Agent Experience Optimization (AXO) & Machine-First Web**
Traditional SEO is dead, replaced by Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) and Agent Experience Optimization (AXO) [axo-and-agentic-infrastructure-strategy](concepts/axo-and-agentic-infrastructure-strategy.md). Over 30 agentic browsers have launched, treating websites not as visual destinations but as data APIs. Chrome 146's early preview of **WebMCP** allows websites to expose native "Tool Contracts" directly to browser AI agents [webmcp-browser-agent-standards](concepts/webmcp-browser-agent-standards.md). B2B marketing tech is pivoting to track "AI Visibility" and citation metrics inside LLM latent spaces, creating lucrative opportunities for "Agentic SEO" micro-SaaS audits.

## Open Questions / Counter-Evidence

*   **A2A Scaling Walls & The MQTT Shift:** While the A2A protocol works well for simple delegations, its direct HTTP/gRPC request-response architecture causes severe N-squared connectivity explosions in large swarms. Complex enterprise and Industrial AI architects are pivoting to decoupled, event-driven message brokers (like MQTT 5 and Unified Namespace architectures) to manage asynchronous swarm telemetry without the system collapsing under its own cross-talk [a2a-agent-coordination-protocol](concepts/a2a-agent-coordination-protocol.md).
*   **The MCP "Operational Safety" Gap:** Naked MCP deployments are a catastrophic vulnerability waiting to happen. The recent "LiteLLM proxy breach" proved that agent orchestration middleware is a primary target for supply chain credential theft. In high-stakes environments (like network hardware or industrial ops), standard MCP is being blocked in favor of strict "Beyond MCP" zero-trust implementations utilizing Just-In-Time (JIT) SSH certs and scope-based JWTs [secure-mcp-infrastructure-gateway](concepts/secure-mcp-infrastructure-gateway.md). 
*   **Benchmark Contamination Crisis:** Public evaluations have become functionally useless. Evidence of models (e.g., Claude 4.6) "gaming" benchmarks by recognizing eval data sets has sparked an integrity crisis. The industry is being forced back to proprietary, spec-driven development (e.g., GSD Framework) and closed-loop, deterministic UI testing [mcp-agentic-infrastructure-2026](recent/2026-03/mcp-agentic-infrastructure-2026.md).
*   **Audit Trail Deficiencies in "Computer Use" Agents:** While vision-driven desktop automation unlocks legacy workflows without APIs, it introduces massive compliance friction. Because "Computer Use" relies on pixel coordinate clicks, it bypasses granular application-level tracking. Current OS audit trails cannot differentiate a human user's click from an agent's click, stalling deployment in highly regulated environments like banking and legal [agentic-authorization-infrastructure](concepts/agentic-authorization-infrastructure.md).
