## Recent Developments

*   **Browser-Native AI Execution (Chrome 146):** Google and Microsoft previewed WebMCP (`navigator.modelContext`). Websites can now expose structured tools directly to built-in browser agents, eliminating brittle screen-scraping and unlocking highly reliable web automation workflows.
*   **"Headless SaaS" via MCP Apps:** Anthropic introduced SEP-1865, enabling third-party tools to inject sandboxed, interactive HTML/JS interfaces directly into Claude. This shifts SaaS development toward building backend utility while relying entirely on AI clients for user acquisition and workflow housing.
*   **Agentic Trust & Cryptographic Authorization:** Mastercard and Google open-sourced a "Verifiable Intent" spec using three-layer SD-JWT chains. This solves the failure of traditional app-first OAuth by allowing autonomous agents to cryptographically prove exact user authorizations to third parties (critical for fintech/legal moats).
*   **Overcoming the Enterprise "SSO Wall":** Gateways like WorkOS, Scalekit, and Zuplo released dedicated security infrastructure for agents, including Client-Initiated Model Discovery (CIMD) and Scoped Auth. This provides the necessary B2B compliance moat for AI startups to clear enterprise procurement.
*   **The Shift from SEO to AXO:** With Google patent US 12536233B1 enabling search engines to bypass low-quality landing pages, Agent Experience Optimization (AXO) is replacing SEO. Cloudflare introduced "Markdown for Agents" to reduce crawler token overhead by 80% through "Machine Comfort Bias."
*   **Terminal Agency & Scheduled Workflows:** CLI agents (like Claude Code) are moving from simple code generation to autonomous DevOps. Native integration of CRON-like tasks enables persistent, background-running "Scouts" capable of local read/write/execute operations.

## Trending Now

*   **SaaS UI Decoupling:** The application layer is dissolving into the orchestrator. Startups are increasingly building highly specific backend workflows (via MCP) and abandoning bespoke frontend dashboards, leaning on centralized chat interfaces to render their UIs dynamically.
*   **Infrastructure for High-Stakes Action:** The ecosystem is rapidly migrating from "chatting with data" to taking high-stakes actions in regulated environments. The proliferation of agentic gateways, Verifiable Intent, and CIMD indicates that enterprise compliance and secure agent-to-agent authorization are becoming the strongest venture moats.
*   **Agent-Optimized Internet:** The web is being bifurcated into human-readable and agent-parsable planes. Optimizing for LLM ingestion, verifiability, and structured tool exposure (WebMCP/AXO) is now more critical for distribution than traditional search rankings.
*   **Asynchronous & Persistent Autonomy:** The market is transitioning away from synchronous, prompt-response loops toward asynchronous, scheduled workflows where AI acts as a persistent digital employee executing background DevOps, research, or pipeline management.
